# Juliana Herbert

Professor and researcher in **Biomedical Software Engineering**, **Digital Health**, and **Sustainable Software Systems**.

I am a faculty member at the **Federal University of Health Sciences of Porto Alegre (UFCSPA), Brazil**, and founder of the **SEEDS – Sustainability in Biomedical Software Engineering** research initiative.

My work focuses on advancing **software engineering methods, models, and infrastructures for sustainable digital health systems**, integrating software engineering with sustainability, artificial intelligence, and healthcare technologies.



## Research agenda

My research explores how **software engineering can support the long-term sustainability of digital health systems**, addressing technical, social, economic, environmental, and individual dimensions of sustainability.

Current research themes include:

- Sustainable Software Engineering
- Biomedical Software Systems
- Digital Health and Health Informatics
- Evidence-Based Software Engineering
- Sustainability in Software Quality and Testing
- Sustainability in Requirements Engineering



## SEEDS – Sustainability in Biomedical Software Engineering

SEEDS is a research initiative focused on integrating **sustainability principles into software engineering**, particularly in the context of **digital health systems**.

The initiative develops:

- sustainability evaluation models
- maturity models and indicators
- sustainable software engineering practices
- tools for digital health systems

🌱 https://seeds.ufcspa.edu.br
